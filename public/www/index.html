The Apartment

My wife and I lived here for just under two years and absolutely loved it. The only reason we're leaving is because we bought a house. So now we're looking for new tenants to take over our lease. This is a Re-let and not a sublease, which is to say you'll be starting a new lease with the owner / management company.

The apartment is huge, at around 1,400 square feet. The master bedroom is enormous - we didn't have enough furniture to fill it (even with a King size Bed), and the second bedroom was able to fit a queen size bed and my office (full size desk and executive office chair) with plenty of room to spare. Both bedrooms have their own closets and windows.

The bathroom is a fairly normal size and the water pressure in the shower is outstanding. The apartment has its own water-heater, so no unexpected screaming in the mornings.

Our favorite aspect of this place is how the kitchen, dining room, and living room are all connected, making for a great space to have guests. The kitchen is not large - more like an extension of the dining room - but it's workable with a fridge, dishwasher, small counter-top and just enough cabinets. There are also pocket doors leading to the Living Room which is smaller than the Dining Room, but was able to fit a full size couch, love seat, and television stand.

The apartment also has its own laundry room with a washer, dryer, and sink. The laundry room is separate, but it's not shared. The two other apartments in the building have their own laundry.

The lighting around the apartment is substantial. There's dimmable track lighting in the living room, overhead and track lighting in the dining room, and overhead lighting in the kitchen. There's also plenty of light from the nine tall, deep windows around the apartment. The windows reach to the ceiling with deep sills for plants and display items.

There is Forced-Air Heating and Air Conditioning with two thermostats - one for the front of the apartment (kitchen / dining / living) and one for the bedrooms / bathroom.

The rent also includes an uncovered parking spot in a private lot behind the building - one for each apartment in the building, with plenty of space for each car. Otherwise, there's metered parking on Augusta and zoned parking on the side street.

The Location

The location is absolutely perfect. It's just one block away from the new Dollop Bakeshop, The Winchester (restaurant), Briciola, Greek Corner, Francesca’s Cafe, Innertown Pub, and Ola's. Within six blocks in any direction, there is a Mariano's, at least twenty amazing bars and restaurants, and plenty of great shops, salons, dry cleaners, etc. North and Damen is just a fifteen minute walk away. We rarely had to drive to run errands or to entertain visitors as there's so much to do within walking distance. You can even walk to Oak Street Beach on a nice day, which is about an hour walk.

The best part of where this apartment is located is that it's really close to lots of things, without being in the middle of it; so you can enjoy the festivals on Division (two blocks away), and then come home unhindered by blocked-off streets and crowds. The Blue line (Damen or Division) is a 15-minute walk away, and the 90/94 is 4 minutes away.

If we could have afforded a house in this neighborhood, we definitely would have stayed right where we were.
